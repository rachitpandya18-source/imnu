Institute of Management Nirma University

The Institute of Management at Nirma University, Ahmedabad, is a well-established business school founded in 1996. It focuses on developing future managers and leaders with a practical understanding of business and society. The institute offers full-time MBA and Integrated BBA-MBA programs along with specialisations in areas such as Finance, Marketing, Human Resource Management, Operations, Analytics and International Business. Programs are designed with industry-oriented curriculum and real-world learning experiences to prepare students for global careers.
